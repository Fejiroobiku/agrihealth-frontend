Our Story

Founded in 2020 by agricultural health professionals, AgriHealth Africa emerged from witnessing firsthand the health challenges faced by smallholder farmers across the continent.

We recognized that while farmers work hard to feed nations, their own health often suffers due to limited access to relevant, practical health information.

Our platform bridges this gap by delivering tailored health education in multiple languages through mobile-friendly formats.

Our Mission

To revolutionize agricultural health by equipping African farmers with life-changing knowledge and tools. Through culturally-sensitive, multilingual resources delivered via innovative low-bandwidth platforms, we're tackling preventable health issues from pesticide exposure to malnutrition.

(Expanded in 2023 to include livestock health initiatives)

Our Vision

We envision a thriving Africa where agricultural health equity eliminates preventable diseases among farmers. By 2030, we aim to establish regional health knowledge hubs in every African nation, creating sustainable ecosystems where farmers can access - and contribute to - life-saving health information.

(Recognized by World Health Organization as an innovative approach)

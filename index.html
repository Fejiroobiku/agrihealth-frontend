Daily Health Tips

Practical advice for African farmers

Sun Protection

Wear wide-brimmed hats and long-sleeved clothing to protect against sunburn and heat stroke. Apply shea butter or other natural moisturizers to protect exposed skin.

Relevant for: All regions
